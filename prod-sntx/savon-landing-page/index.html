Synopsis

Savon single release is reminiscent of the vibes of the old times. Something that started in the Punk Era, and ended up in commercial night clubs.

Soap, French for soap, is what makes the city so slippery, according to some guy on the Warschauer Bruecke.

As continuation of the music track ,this dedicated landing page serves to extend the song as an interactive experience, around the theme of liquid harmonics, and soapy acoustics.

Notice

This demo requires a decent graphics card and up-to-date drivers. If you can't run the demo, you can still see it on YouTube.
